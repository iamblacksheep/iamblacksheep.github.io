The Beginning

They say everyone is good at something. Good at cooking? Talented Musician? Professional Teacher? or an Expert?

Well, in this site. I would like to take a glance on what the world has to offer, what the internet has to offer. By the means of discovering what they have to offer. I would like to answer the question on what is a person good at. What is his talent, his expertise or his savvy-ness.

Through this project, maybe I could find what I'm really good at. What makes me wake up every morning and keeps me up at night like not wanting to sleep it off. What's my passion?

We all need to seek our passion, or else we would be living an unfulfilled life ahead of us. Now, do you ever ask the question: What is my passion in life? What am I good at?
